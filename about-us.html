History e-farm

Organic & Healthy Food

We are a Section-8 Company registered under the Companies Act 2013. Our headquarter is in Bangalore while our Hydroponic Farm is located at Jawadhu Hills in the Tiruvannamalai District of Tamil Nadu. More than 80% of the tribal communities in this region live below Poverty Line. The Hydroponic Farm aims to help boost local economy by creating jobs and promoting entrepreneurship.
